## Filed Patents

### 1. Quantum-Optimized Personalized Wellness Routines

**Application Number**: USXX/XXX,XXX  
**Filed**: 2026-02-05  
**Status**: Pending

**Abstract**: 
Methods and systems for optimizing personal wellness routines using quantum approximate optimization algorithms (QAOA). The system receives user constraints (time, energy, preferences) and objectives (wellness activities), formulates a combinatorial optimization problem, and solves it using quantum computing resources.

**Claims**:
1. A method comprising: receiving wellness routine constraints; formulating as QUBO problem; submitting to quantum processor; returning optimized schedule.
2. The method of claim 1, where the quantum processor is selected from IBM Quantum, AWS Braket, or Google Sycamore.
3. The method of claim 1, further comprising classical verification of quantum results.

**Innovation**: First application of QAOA to daily wellness scheduling.

### 2. Post-Quantum Cryptography for Health Data Protection

**Application Number**: USXX/XXX,XXX  
**Filed**: 2026-02-05  
**Status**: Pending

**Abstract**:
Systems and methods for protecting health and wellness data using post-quantum cryptographic algorithms including CRYSTALS-Dilithium, SPHINCS+, and NTRU. The system generates hybrid classical/post-quantum keypairs, signs transactions with post-quantum signatures, and verifies data integrity against quantum attacks.

**Claims**:
1. A data protection system comprising: wellness data storage; post-quantum key generation; hybrid signature creation; blockchain anchoring.
2. The system of claim 1, where the post-quantum algorithm is CRYSTALS-Dilithium.
3. The system of claim 1, further comprising quantum-resistant identity registry.

**Innovation**: First wellness platform with NIST-standardized post-quantum security.
